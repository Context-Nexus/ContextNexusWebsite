Frequently Asked Questions

Core Product Usage

Do you provide the AI?

No. Context Nexus is a developer tool. You connect your own AI account (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or a custom endpoint).

Which AI providers are supported?

OpenAI (ChatGPT / GPT models), Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, plus a Custom Endpoint option for OpenAI-compatible APIs.

ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude are trademarks of their respective owners. Context Nexus is not affiliated with or endorsed by OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic.

Can I use local or self-hosted AI models?

Yes. Use Custom Endpoint to connect to OpenAI-compatible servers (e.g., Ollama, LM Studio, or your own inference endpoint).

Which Unreal Engine versions are supported?

UE 5.1 – 5.4 (tested). We’ll expand support as new versions release.

Which operating systems are supported?

Windows x64. Additional platforms are being evaluated.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes. Requests are sent from your editor to the AI provider you choose. For local/self-hosted endpoints, your machine must reach that server.

Does Context Nexus work with both Blueprints and C++?

Yes. Our Dual-Source Analysis understands C++ and Blueprints together, including their relationships.

Can I use it in a team project?

Yes. Each developer uses their own license and connects their own AI key.

Pricing & Licensing

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 7-day free trial. After that, upgrade to Pro or Enterprise to continue.

How does licensing work?

Key-based activation, per seat (one user per key). You can move a seat by deactivating and re-activating.

Do I need a subscription to the AI provider too?

Yes. You pay your AI provider directly (usage-based). Context Nexus is the tool; the AI is your account.

Privacy & Security

Do you store my AI key?

No. Your key stays on your machine in editor settings. We don’t store or transmit it to our servers.

Do you log my code or prompts?

No. Only the Targeted Context you explicitly select is sent to your chosen provider to answer your question.

Are you affiliated with OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic?

No. We integrate with their public APIs. No partnership or endorsement is implied.

Features & Workflow

What’s a “Deep Scan”?

Global Analysis that maps your project (C++ + Blueprints) into a knowledge graph.

What is “Targeted Context”?

The minimal, hand-selected slice (files / functions / variables) we send to the AI for your question — boosting accuracy and reducing token cost.

Does it trace Blueprint events like BeginPlay or ReceiveTick?

Yes. Our Ubergraph resolution follows event stubs to the actual executing logic.

Is there a linter?

Yes. The Static Analysis Linter surfaces potential issues and high-cost nodes directly in the UI.

Roadmap

What’s next for Context Nexus?

A Clang AST sidecar for compiler-grade parsing, deeper dependency analysis, and expanded provider support.

Will you support fully offline AI?

We’re exploring local/offline modes via compatible endpoints. Not guaranteed yet, but it’s on our radar.
