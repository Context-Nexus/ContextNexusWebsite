Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 08, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Context Nexus (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our website, services, and plugins (collectively, the “Services”).

1) Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you purchase a license or subscribe, our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy) collects your name, email, and payment details. We receive access to your name, email, and license information.
  • Newsletter: If you subscribe to our newsletter, our email provider (Buttondown) collects your email address.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about your interaction with our website (e.g., pages visited) to improve our Services.
  • Plugin Data: The plugin sends periodic, anonymous pings for license validation. We do not collect or store your source code, AI API keys, prompts, or AI-generated responses.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • To process transactions and manage licenses.
  • To communicate with you, including sending service-related emails and newsletters (if you opt-in).
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security.

3) Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate our business:

  • Lemon Squeezy: Our Merchant of Record for payments and licensing. See their Privacy Policy.
  • Buttondown: For our email newsletter. See their Privacy Policy.

Your AI API key and prompts are sent directly from the plugin on your machine to the AI provider you configure (e.g., OpenAI, Google, Anthropic). We never see or store this data. Your use of those AI services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

4) Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5) Your Rights

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, subject to local law. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

6) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7) Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at support@contextnexus.dev.

Context Nexus provides developer tooling. You connect your own AI account. We never store your API keys.
