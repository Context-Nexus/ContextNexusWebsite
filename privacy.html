Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 08, 2025

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what Personal Data we collect when you use the Context Nexus website (contextnexus.dev) and our plugin(s), how we use and share it, and the choices you have. “Personal Data” means any information that identifies or can be used to identify you.

1) Who is the data controller?

Context Nexus is the data controller for data collected via our Site and plugin(s). For some activities we act as a joint or independent controller with third parties (e.g., Lemon Squeezy as Merchant of Record). You can contact us at privacy@contextnexus.dev.

2) What we collect

  • Contact data: email address if you subscribe to our newsletter (via Buttondown) or contact us.
  • Purchase and subscription data: order details, license/entitlement status, and related metadata processed by Lemon Squeezy; we receive necessary information to manage your subscription/license.
  • Plugin usage/licensing data (if Pro features are gated): activation status, license key (hashed or tokenized), basic device/application identifiers sufficient to validate legitimate use, and timestamps of activation/validation.
  • Communications: messages you post in our discussion channels (e.g., GitHub Discussions/Discord) and messages you send to us.
  • Site logs: basic server logs (IP address, user‑agent, referrer) for security and troubleshooting.

3) How we use your data

  • Provide and maintain the Services (website, plugin functionality, license activation and validation).
  • Process purchases, subscriptions, invoices, and customer support (via Lemon Squeezy).
  • Send you product updates and newsletters if you opt in (via Buttondown). You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • Protect, investigate, and deter against fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements).

4) Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract: to provide the Services you request (e.g., your subscription/license).
  • Consent: for the newsletter and certain cookies/marketing. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure our Services, prevent abuse, and improve functionality.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with tax and accounting rules.

5) Sharing and processors

We share Personal Data with service providers/“processors” who help us operate the Services, and with “controllers” when necessary:

  • Lemon Squeezy (controller/processor): payments, subscriptions, licenses, tax handling, and invoicing.
  • Buttondown (processor): newsletter email collection, delivery, and unsubscribe management.
  • Discussion platforms (controller): GitHub Discussions (via Giscus) and/or Discord for community conversations. Your use of those platforms is subject to their privacy policies.
  • Hosting and infrastructure: web hosting, storage, and security providers we use to run the Site and backend.
  • Compliance and safety: we may disclose information to comply with law, protect rights, or respond to lawful requests.

6) International transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Where applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses for international transfers.

7) Data retention

We keep Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, including to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Newsletter emails are retained until you unsubscribe or we delete the list. Transaction records may be retained to meet tax/accounting requirements.

8) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your Personal Data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise rights, contact us at privacy@contextnexus.dev. You can also unsubscribe from the newsletter via the link in any email.

9) Cookies and tracking

We use only minimal cookies necessary to operate the Site and remember preferences, and server‑side logs for security. If we add analytics or marketing cookies in the future, we will update this Policy and, where required, request consent.

10) Children’s privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. If you believe a child provided us Personal Data, please contact us so we can delete it.

11) Third‑party links

The Site may link to third‑party sites/services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. We are not responsible for their content or practices.

12) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated Policy on the Site with a new effective date. Your continued use of the Services after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.

13) Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at privacy@contextnexus.dev.
